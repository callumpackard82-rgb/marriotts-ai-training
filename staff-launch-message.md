## Version B — Email to all staff (fuller)

**Subject: Get confident with AI — our new self-paced Staff AI Programme is live**

Dear colleagues,

AI tools are now part of the landscape, and the questions I hear most are *"where do I even start?"* and *"what's actually safe to use?"* — so I've put together a Staff AI Programme to answer both, built for our school rather than generic training.

**It meets you where you are.** There are three pathways, and you choose:

- **Foundations** — for anyone curious or sceptical: what generative AI really is, what it's genuinely useful for in your role, and how to prompt it well.
- **Practitioner** — for confident users wanting to get good: choosing the right tool, generating images, using research modes, and building your own reusable assistants.
- **Builder** — for those who want to make things: from quick interactive tools to small apps, shared responsibly.

**A few things that make it easy to start:**

- Each module is about **15 minutes** with a clear, hands-on task — no theory dumps.
- You pick **your preferred tool** (Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT or Gemini) and the guidance adapts.
- Your **progress is saved** so you can dip in and out.
- **Data protection is built into every module.** The simple rule throughout: never put identifiable pupil or staff information into an AI tool the school hasn't approved — and for any real school data, use **Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat** signed in with your school account (look for the green shield). Each module reinforces this.

You don't need to do it all at once — just open **Foundations** and try the first module. If you've got questions or want to feed back as you go, I'd love to hear from you.
