🛡️ One rule runs through everything

Never put identifiable pupil or staff data into an AI tool the school hasn't approved for it. For real school data, use Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat, signed in with your school account, with the green shield showing. Every module teaches the two-second traffic-light check:

🟢 Generic — any tool 🟡 Real but minimised — Copilot, green shield 🔴 Sensitive — not an AI task
